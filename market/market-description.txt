Have your Vertcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Vertcoin Wallet is the first mobile Vertcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Vertcoin amount in VTC and mVTC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Vertcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Vertcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Vertcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Vertcoin balance.

You can find more about Vertcoin at
http://www.vertcoin.org/

If you want to contribute to Vertcoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/dayreiner/Vertcoin-wallet
